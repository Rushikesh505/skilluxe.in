COURSE DETAILS: MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)

WHAT DOES AN MBBS CAREER ENTAIL?

An MBBS degree prepares students for a career in medicine, where they will diagnose and treat patients, perform medical procedures, and contribute to healthcare research. MBBS graduates can work in various fields such as clinical practice, medical research, public health, and healthcare administration.

COURSES REQUIRED TO PURSUE:
  • 📘 BIPC (Biology, Physics, Chemistry)
  • 📅 NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)
HOW TO ACHIEVE AN MBBS CAREER:
  • 1. Complete Higher Secondary Education (10+2) with BIPC subjects.
  • 2. Prepare for and clear the NEET exam.
  • 3. Enroll in a recognized MBBS program.
  • 4. Complete the MBBS degree (5.5 years including internship).
  • 5. Complete a compulsory rotating internship in a recognized hospital.
  • 6. Obtain a license to practice medicine by registering with the Medical Council.
EXTERNAL RESOURCES:
  • Introduction to MBBS Course
  • Preparing for NEET Exam
COMPETITIVE EXAMS:
  • 📅 NEET
  • 📅 AIIMS
  • 📅 JIPMER
CAREER OPPORTUNITIES:
  • 🏥 General Practitioner
  • 🏥 Specialist (e.g., surgeon, pediatrician, cardiologist)
  • 🏥 Researcher in medical science
  • 🏥 Public Health Administrator
  • 🏥 Medical Officer in armed forces or government health services
SKILLS REQUIRED:
  • 🩺 Strong Analytical Skills
  • 🩺 Excellent Communication Skills
  • 🩺 Empathy and Compassion
  • 🩺 Ability to Work Under Pressure
  • 🩺 Attention to Detail
SALARY PROSPECTS:

The salary for MBBS graduates can vary widely based on specialization, experience, and location. On average, an MBBS doctor can earn:

  • 💰 Fresh graduates: ₹6,00,000 - ₹12,00,000 per annum
  • 💰 Experienced doctors: ₹12,00,000 - ₹30,00,000 per annum
  • 💰 Specialists: ₹15,00,000 - ₹50,00,000 per annum
